Questions

Asked, answered.

What is global life arbitrage?

Life arbitrage is relocating your existing skills to the country where they command the highest quality of life — net salary, visa access, healthcare, and cost of living combined. BORNE quantifies that gap across 21 countries and calls it your Born Wrong index.

How does BORNE apply to jobs for me?

Drop one resume. BORNE parses it, ranks 21 countries against your profile and preferences, then its autonomous pipeline scrapes live openings, filters for visa-sponsorship fit, tailors your resume and cover letter per role, and packages applications — while you watch the stream in real time.

Is BORNE really free?

Yes. Guest mode gives you one full autonomous run with zero signup. A free account adds a second run. Pro unlocks unlimited runs. The first 50 users get founder pricing for life.
