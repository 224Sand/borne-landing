Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-11

BORNE ("we", "us") operates the BORNE platform. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights.

What we collect

  • Account data — email address and authentication tokens.
  • Profile data — your resume and the preferences you provide. This may include personal and professional details you choose to share.
  • Usage data — pipeline runs, generated applications, and feature usage.

Why we collect it

  • To compute your country rankings, visa pathways, and salary analysis (legitimate interest / contract performance).
  • To generate tailored job application materials at your request.
  • To authenticate you and prevent abuse.

AI processing

Your resume and preferences are processed by third-party large-language-model providers (e.g. Anthropic) to generate analysis and application materials. We send only what is necessary for the feature you invoke. We do not sell your data, ever.

Retention & your rights (GDPR)

  • You may request access, export, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting hello@borne.app.
  • Guest session data is ephemeral and pruned on a rolling basis.
  • We retain account data only while your account is active.

Cookies & analytics

We use no third-party advertising cookies. Authentication uses local storage tokens. Any analytics we run are cookieless and aggregate.

Contact

Data questions: hello@borne.app
